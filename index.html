Description: This project represents pages digitized from the A.B Wiskadals Fabric catalog of Fall/Winter of 1951-1952. The physical copy of the catalog is currently owned by the Textile Museum of Borås. The catalog includes various categories of items like womenswear, menswear, kidswear, fabrics, toys, home decor and crockery for purchase. For this project the pages 40 and 41 have been digitized and encoded.
