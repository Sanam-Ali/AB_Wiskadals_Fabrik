Description: A hardcover bound copy of the Autumn-Winter 1951-52 clothing catalog published by Borås clothing and homeware company AB Wiskadals Fabrik, bound for company director Sara Häggström at an unknown date and supplied to the authors of this document courtesy of Borås Textile Musuem. The catalog various categories of items like womenswear, menswear, kidswear, fabrics, toys, home decor and crockery for purchase.
